# Exno.9-To explore and understand the various prompting techniques used for generating videos through AI models. 

#### Date: 22.11.2025
#### Register no.: 212223060023

## **Aim**

To explore and understand the various prompting techniques used for generating videos using AI models.

---

## **Algorithm**

1. Familiarize yourself with video generation models.
2. Create simple prompts for video generation.
3. Experiment with detailed prompts.
4. Add time and motion elements.
5. Test different video styles.
6. Iterate and adjust prompts.
7. Generate multiple versions.
8. Save and compare outputs.

---

## **Procedure**

### **1. Familiarize Yourself with Video Generation Models**

Explore AI tools capable of generating videos from text prompts:

* Runway Gen-2
* Synthesia
* Pictory
* DeepBrain AI

Understand their capabilities, limitations, output duration, style options, and supported features before starting.

---
